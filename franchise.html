Why Tea Room Franchise?

As Tea Room continues to expand around the globe day by day, we are looking for passionate and motivated entrepreneurs to join our franchisees.Our vision is to reach to all tea lovers and make them taste the best variants of tea globally.You can take the role of an ambassador in promoting our culture of tea and help in serving our customers with premium milkshakes,coolers nd etc. Join us today!!

Franchise Requirements


✓ Minimum 100 sqft outlet at a busy location which can be near to bus stops, highways, hospitals, markets, railway stations, IT campus, colleges etc.

✓ The outlet should have tile flooring, kitchen partition, water connection & an outlet counter. Franchisee has to arrange the said ones only. The rest of all the brand will take care of.

✓ Get 2 employees who will be trained by our chef to run your business. Anybody can learn the entire TeaRoom menu in 3 days.

✓ Anyone can afford to start the TeaRoom franchise In the franchise fee it self, We offer marketing and the complete start up kit consists of the movable equipments
(Refrigerator, Deep Freezer, Stoves, Utensils etc) & an initial stock to start the business. So, anyone can start this business without hesitation.
